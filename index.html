My "campaign promises" / ideas

There are a couple of things I have noticed from when I started in SHM, with some private interactions, and from my own observations in HQ that I believe are issues with TCN. Some are not that serious like ban-share vulnerabilities, inconsistent standards, or a feeling that some ban-shares are uncalled for, unreasonable, or due to bad moderators on other servers, but some are more serious like a sense of TCN being pretentious or egotistical or petty, trying to hold a monopoly on Genshin Mains servers and instating their own ideals, etc.

  • I believe that we need a TCN server for people who want to know about decisions and changes that happen, or a news channel to keep people updated.
  • Changes to policy, ongoing projects, and the reason for decisions like new executives/observers, servers being induced (and maybe starting observation or being rejected, depending on how much that can be public), constitution changes, etc. should all be announced and not silently done.
  • It’s impossible not to be biased, but I’ve seen concerns or mentions of cronyism / heavy advantages for being part of the same friend group or a clique-y feel from TCN, and I think it’s important that we are transparent with our decisions and give reasoning for our choices to avoid this appearance.
  • Decisions take way too long to happen and it seems like not much is changing (after alpha’s “do anything” vote suggestion nearly a month ago, I haven’t really seen all that much been done despite a lot of discussion about all sorts of ideas), so as an executive, I would set clear timelines for new ideas and introduce deadlines and a clear action plan to make things actually happen.
